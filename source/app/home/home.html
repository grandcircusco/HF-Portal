Welcome!

Fellows:

Congratulations! You have all made it through the rounds, and are officially Hackerfellows! In the next phases you all will interview with various start-ups to find a match, complete the 5 week coding bootcamp, and then begin working for your companies. The purpose of this website is to serve as the first method interaction with the companies. You will be able to view basic information about the various companies, and vote on the 5 that you are interested in.
